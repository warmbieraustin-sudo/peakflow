# Privacy Policy - PeakFlow

**Effective Date:** March 9, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 9, 2026

## Overview

PeakFlow ("we", "our", or "us") is an AI-powered training platform for endurance athletes currently in early development. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

## Development Status

PeakFlow is currently in **alpha development** with a single user (the developer). This policy will be updated before any public release or beta testing phase.

## Information We Collect

When you use PeakFlow, we may collect:

### From Garmin Connect
- Health metrics (heart rate, HRV, sleep data, body battery)
- Activity data (workouts, power, pace, duration)
- Weight and body composition data
- Device information

### From Other Integrations
- WHOOP: Recovery scores, sleep, strain data
- Strava: Activity summaries and workout streams
- TrainingPeaks: Prescribed workouts and training plans (if connected)

### Account Information
- Email address
- Name
- Authentication tokens (securely stored)

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

- Generate personalized training insights and recommendations
- Analyze workout performance and recovery trends
- Provide adaptive training plan adjustments
- Calculate fitness metrics (TSS, CTL/ATL/TSB, power analysis)
- Improve our AI models and algorithms

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
- Authentication tokens are encrypted at rest
- We implement secure OAuth 2.0 flows for third-party integrations
- Database access is restricted and logged

## Data Sharing

We **do not**:
- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Share your health data with advertisers
- Use your data for purposes other than providing PeakFlow services

We **may** share anonymized, aggregated data for research or product improvement purposes.

## Third-Party Services

PeakFlow integrates with:
- **Garmin Connect** - subject to [Garmin Privacy Policy](https://www.garmin.com/en-US/privacy/)
- **WHOOP** - subject to [WHOOP Privacy Policy](https://www.whoop.com/privacy/)
- **Strava** - subject to [Strava Privacy Policy](https://www.strava.com/legal/privacy)
- **TrainingPeaks** - subject to [TrainingPeaks Privacy Policy](https://www.trainingpeaks.com/privacy-policy/)

Your use of these services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data at any time
- **Delete** your account and all associated data
- **Export** your data in a portable format
- **Revoke** third-party integrations
- **Opt-out** of data collection (by disconnecting integrations)

## Data Retention

- We retain your data for as long as your account is active
- You may request deletion at any time
- Deleted data is permanently removed within 30 days

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as PeakFlow evolves. We will notify users of any material changes via email or in-app notification before they take effect.
