# PeakFlow - Vision

## What We're Building
An AI endurance coach that adapts your training plan in real-time based on recovery data, execution performance, and subjective feedback.

## Core Features
- **Multi-source data integration:** WHOOP, Garmin, Strava, Apple Health
- **Autonomous plan generation:** Weekly training plans with structured workouts
- **Real-time adaptation:** Swap workouts based on HRV, sleep, body battery
- **Post-workout feedback loop:** Learn from how you felt, adjust future sessions
- **Conversational interface:** Chat with your coach, ask questions, get explanations

## The Gap We're Filling
Current tools either:
- Static plans (TrainerRoad, TrainingPeaks) that don't adapt to YOUR recovery
- Human coaches ($200-500/mo) that are slow to adjust
- Generic AI advice without live data integration

PeakFlow: Real-time AI coaching for $20-50/mo.

## Target Market
- Serious self-coached athletes (30-50% of endurance market)
- Coached athletes looking to supplement ($10-20/mo add-on)
- 20-35M total addressable users globally

## Success Metrics
- Daily engagement (do they check the app every day?)
- Plan adherence (do they follow workout recommendations?)
- Retention (still using after 90 days?)
